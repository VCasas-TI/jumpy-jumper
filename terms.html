Rental Terms & Conditions

Please read our rental policies carefully before booking. By placing a reservation you agree to these terms.

📅 Reservations

Jumpy-Jumper is open for business Monday through Sunday from 8:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. You may call us at (818) 445-5036 or email us at jumpyjumper62@gmail.com to make a reservation.

💰 Method of Payment

Payment is due in full at the time of set up/delivery. At this time we only accept cash. Please be sure to have the exact change as our drivers do not carry cash or change. No personal checks or money orders are accepted.

🚚 Delivery and Pick-Up

Deliveries start as early as 8:00 a.m. We deliver within 5 miles at no charge. Beyond that, a delivery fee applies depending on distance.

🔧 Setup and Take Down

An adult must be present during the installation and take down of the equipment. Our drivers do not rearrange, move, or clear customer's belongings from driveway or front/backyard. Setting up a jumper takes approximately 20–30 minutes. Water Units require a hose within range of setup; water must be turned off when unit is no longer in use. An electrical outlet must be located within 75 feet of the blowers from the house. The delivery person will provide you with a copy of the rules and review safety procedures with you, and collect payment prior to setting up the unit. We provide portable generators at additional cost for Parks Events.

📋 Tables and Chairs

It is the customer's responsibility to set up and break down tables and chairs.

🌍 Surface

Units can be set up on almost any surface (concrete, asphalt, grass and driveway or cul-de-sac). Please specify and inform our customer service representative where you intend to place the unit. The area must be clear of rocks, sticks, roots, etc. that could puncture the bouncer. Also, if you have an in-ground sprinkler system, make sure it is turned off.

⚠️ Safety Rules

These rules must be followed to avoid serious injury. Supervision by a qualified attendant is required at all times.

  • Remove all shoes, eyeglasses, and sharp objects.
  • Diving, wrestling, rough play, and flips are prohibited.
  • NO food, drinks, gum, pets or SILLY STRINGS.
  • Riders with physical impairment SHOULD NOT be allowed to use the inflatable without supervision of a qualified attendant.
  • EXIT if the unit begins to lose air. USE exit doors and escape hatches.
  • DO NOT use and immediately deflate the inflatable if winds exceed 15 MPH and/or rains.
  • Riders SHOULD all be compatible age and size.
  • The inflatable must be securely tied down at all times.
  • Keep children away from the blower at all times.
  • Bounce away from others and away from the sides.
  • Do not jump on top of the inflatable. Do not climb on.

📉 Our Cancellation Policy

We realize that unforeseen circumstances can arise and you may have to cancel or reschedule your reservation. Our cancellation policy: cancellation must be made 72 hours ahead of the event date by calling us at (818) 445-5036.

Jumpy-Jumper reserves the right to cancel a rental if it is raining and/or if high winds are to be expected. If the local forecast is calling for high winds (25 MPH), or rain on the day of your reservation, we will contact you via telephone to reschedule your reservation.
